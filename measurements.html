What Are Measurements?

We use measurements to find out length, weight, and volume.

  • 📏 Length – Measured in meters (m), centimeters (cm), or inches (in).
  • ⚖️ Weight – Measured in grams (g), kilograms (kg), or pounds (lb).
  • 🥛 Volume – Measured in liters (L) or milliliters (mL).
