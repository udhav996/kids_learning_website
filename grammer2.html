📚 English Grammar Lessons

🎥 What is a Noun?

A noun is a word that names a person, place, thing, or idea.

Examples: Teacher, School, Apple

📝 Quiz

🎥 What are Pronouns?

Pronouns replace nouns. Example: He, She, It, They, We

📝 Quiz

Which one is a pronoun?

🎥 What are Verbs?

Verbs are action words. Example: Run, Eat, Jump

📝 Quiz

Which one is a verb?

🎥 What are Adjectives?

Adjectives describe nouns. Example: Beautiful, Tall, Fast

🎥 What are Articles?

Articles define if something is specific or unspecific. Examples: a, an, the

🎥 What are Prepositions?

Prepositions show location, direction, or time. Example: in, on, under

🎥 Sentence Structure

Basic English sentences follow: Subject + Verb + Object

🎥 What are Tenses?

Tenses show when an action happens: Past, Present, Future
